DECLARATION

We warrant that all foods, goods and packaging supplied by us to Pukka Pies Ltd:-

Comply in all respects with this specification and any description applied will be clean, sound and undamaged on delivery.
Are made from prime quality ingredients and comply in all respects with the Food Safety Act 1990, with any regulations made thereunder and with all other United Kingdom and European legislation, regulations and quality standards relating to the supply of food, including accepted Codes of Practice. Is not injurious to health, is safe and complies with the requirements of the General Product Safety Regulations 2005 and any safety provisions made under the Consumer Protection Act 1987. Will comply with all aspects of General Food Law Regulation (EC) 178/2002 thus ensuring traceability of the product and all the ingredients are sufficient to allow an effective and timely recall/withdrawal. Will be of the nature, substance or quality described and not presented in such a way to mislead as to nature, substance or quality and be free from agricultural and veterinary contaminants. Will have packaging that complies with all legal Food Contact Material requirements, where appropriate.

Have been subject to such checks as a prudent manufacturer would reasonably carry out to meet all the above requirements.
Are produced in premises and with equipment, machinery and other apparatus which comply with the standards of cleanliness and hygiene required by the Food Safety Act and regulations made thereunder. Is covered by Nutritional and Dietary Intolerance information supplied, which will be validated independently at least annually and any interim deviations notified.

Will be free from problems likely to result in consumer dissatisfaction and that Pukka Pies Ltd will be immediately notified should this not be the case. Confirm that we comply with Regulation (EC) No 1829/2003 on genetically modified food & feed, and Regulation (EC) No 1830/2003 concerning the traceability & labelling of genetically modified organisms (GMOs) and the traceability of food & feed products produced from GMOs A pest control contract to be maintained with an approved company.

The ingredients used must comply with the UK DEFRA list for Pesticides, Fungicides and Herbicides etc.
Residual Pesticide levels in the final product must conform to the UK and EEC limits.

Mycotoxins (specifically Ochratoxin A) levels in the final product must conform to the Commission regulation (EU) no 105/2010 where applicable.

There shall be NO DEVIATION from this specification without prior agreement with Pukka Pies Ltd

Pukka Pies Ltd will have access to all technical test data held by the supplier relating to product supplied under this specification.
